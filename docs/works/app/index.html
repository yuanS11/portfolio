UI/UX

A case study focusing on a shampoo cutomizing app. The background being the more and more prevalance problem with hair loss in younger generation.

This is a hair product customizing app. This app consists of a hair quiz, your personal hair records, and a section on tips on hair care.

Users will first take a quiz on their hair condition and hair needs. The app will generate a fully customizable formula based on the quiz results.

The app saves all quiz results and previous purchases for easy re-ordering.

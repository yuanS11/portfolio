UI/UX

A case study focusing on a shampoo cutomizing app. The background being the more and more prevalance problem with hair loss in younger generation.

This app consists of many features. A hair quiz, your personal records, and a tips section.

Users will first take a quiz on their hair conditions and hair needs. The app would then generate a personalized customizable formula for Users.

This app also saves all quiz results and past orders for easy re-ordering.

Poetry

A series of blackout poetries I created. The original text is from the History of Sexuality by Michel Foucault.

Perhaps fear has been the reason to free ourselves, for timidity is repression.

I was formed in the forms of invalidation.

We set out in search of our broken moral confession.

Your words plot the scandalous narration decorated with passion.

But love cannot be tied to reasons.
